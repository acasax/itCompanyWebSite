IT SECTOR - Solutions

Routing and switching

Classic network planning from suggestion of hardware to configuration of switches and routers in the network, from simple to much more demanding networks, it is necessary in every IP based line of work. Optional maintenance after the installation is always available.

Wireless

Wireless site survey for replacing old network or introducing wireless in a new environment – planning and/or adjustments, procurement and configuration of devices, delivery and implementation and at the end - maintenance

Security

LNetwork security services should be designed to protect company networks and connected devices from un-allowed access, mishandling, and attacks. We can offer firewalls and other protective measures that are configured to meet each client’s needs.

VOIP

IP telephony solution for VOIP network in every working environment, connecting whole company over IP, inside of one building or beyond.

DOCSIS

GPON

FTTH technology with all benefits that it carries has to be well planed by engineers who know how to make the best solution that will provide optimal service to the customers. From OLT in headend to CPE devices at end-user premisis, we can cover everything. Huawei and ZTE are most represented, but we also offer OEM solutions for ONU/ONT which are compatible with these vendors.

IT Computing

Data centers

Our engineers work with all Windows technologies and Linux platforms, with high level of virtualization to provide the most quality servise to our customers.
